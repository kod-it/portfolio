#### Overview  
E-Quote is a powerful quotation management system developed for **Zoplar**, specifically tailored to streamline the process of generating quotations for medical equipment. This tech-driven solution ensures precision, efficiency, and a seamless experience for both businesses and their customers.  

#### Key Features  

- **Custom Quotation Management**  
  - Switch between collapsed and expanded views for clear and detailed price breakdowns.  
  - Modify product quantities and selections effortlessly through a user-friendly interface.  
  - Bulk actions like "Select/Unselect All" for quick adjustments to large product lists.  

- **Professional PDF Generation**  
  - Generate downloadable PDFs of quotes, complete with attachments, ensuring professional documentation for medical equipment quotations.  

- **Streamlined Order & Payment Workflow**  
  - Advanced features to display payment terms, including percentage-based advance payments.  
  - OTP-secured order placement and real-time notifications via SMS and WhatsApp for enhanced communication.  

- **Flexible and Secure Payments**  
  - Customers can choose between full or partial payments, with dynamic options tailored to the transaction amount.  
  - UPI-enabled payments for transactions below â‚¹1,00,000 and comprehensive payment gateways for larger amounts.  
  - Real-time tracking of payment history, remaining dues, and total amounts paid.  

- **Enhanced Communication**  
  - Integration with **Gupshup** and **WATI API** ensures real-time updates and seamless communication with customers via SMS and WhatsApp.  

#### Tech Stack  

- **Frontend**: React.js with libraries like React-PDF-Renderer for advanced PDF generation.  
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js for robust server-side management.  
- **Database**: SQL for efficient data storage and activity tracking.  
- **Payment Gateway**: Razorpay for secure transactions.  
- **Encryption**: AES encryption for secure sharing of E-Quote links.  

#### Advanced Features  

- **Sentiment Tracking**: Leveraging analytics to assess customer feedback and improve service quality.  
- **Activity Logs**: SQL-based tracking for detailed monitoring of user interactions.

E-Quote was specifically developed to address the unique challenges of managing quotations for medical equipment. By integrating cutting-edge technology and a user-friendly interface, it simplifies complex workflows, improves operational efficiency, and enhances customer satisfaction.
